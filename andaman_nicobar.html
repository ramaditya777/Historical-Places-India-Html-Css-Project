Historical Places in Andaman and Nicobar Islands

  • Cellular Jail
  • Ross Island
  • Andaman and Nicobar Islands Museum

Cellular Jail

Cellular Jail, located in Port Blair, is a historical prison used by the British to exile political prisoners. Explore the colonial-era jail, learn about India's struggle for independence, and witness the light and sound show.

Ross Island

Ross Island, also known as Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Island, served as the administrative headquarters during the British colonial era. Explore the ruins, British-era buildings, and enjoy the serene natural beauty.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands Museum

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands Museum in Port Blair showcases the rich cultural and anthropological heritage of the region. Explore artifacts, tribal art, and learn about the islands' diverse history.
