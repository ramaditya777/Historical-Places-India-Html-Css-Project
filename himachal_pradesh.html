Historical Places in Himachal Pradesh

  • Shimla
  • Manali
  • Dharamshala

Shimla

Shimla, the capital city of Himachal Pradesh, has a rich colonial history. The Ridge, Christ Church, and Viceregal Lodge are some of the historical landmarks. Shimla served as the summer capital during the British Raj.

Manali

Manali is known for its ancient temples like Hidimba Devi Temple and Manu Temple. These temples showcase traditional Himachali architecture and are surrounded by breathtaking landscapes.

Dharamshala

Dharamshala, with its historic Kangra Fort, is a significant town in Himachal Pradesh. The Kangra Art Museum and Norbulingka Institute reflect the cultural heritage of the region.
