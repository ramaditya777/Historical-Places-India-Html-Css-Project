Historical Places in Bihar

  • Nalanda University
  • Bodh Gaya - Mahabodhi Temple
  • Patna Sahib
  • Vikramshila University

Nalanda University

Nalanda University was a renowned center of learning in ancient India. It played a significant role in the development of Buddhism and attracted scholars from across the world.

Bodh Gaya - Mahabodhi Temple

Mahabodhi Temple in Bodh Gaya is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the place where Gautama Buddha is said to have attained enlightenment.

Patna Sahib

Patna Sahib, also known as Takht Sri Harmandir Sahib, is one of the Five Takhts in Sikhism. It is the birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh, the tenth Guru of the Sikhs.

Vikramshila University

Vikramshila University was an ancient center of learning in Bihar, founded by King Dharmapala in the late 8th century. It was a major Buddhist learning institution.
