Historical Places in Punjab

  • Golden Temple
  • Jallianwala Bagh
  • Qila Mubarak

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest shrine in Sikhism. Visit the serene surroundings and the iconic golden architecture of this spiritual site.

Jallianwala Bagh

Jallianwala Bagh is a public garden in Amritsar that witnessed a tragic massacre in 1919. Explore the memorial and learn about its historical significance during the Indian independence movement.

Qila Mubarak

Qila Mubarak in Bathinda is an ancient fort with a rich history dating back to the Kushan dynasty. Discover the architectural marvels and historical artifacts within its walls.
