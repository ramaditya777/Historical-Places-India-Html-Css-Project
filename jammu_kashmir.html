Historical Places in Jammu and Kashmir

  • Srinagar
  • Jammu
  • Gulmarg

Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, boasts historic sites like Dal Lake, Jamia Masjid, and Shankaracharya Temple. The Mughal Gardens, including Shalimar Bagh, are iconic landmarks.

Jammu

Jammu is known for its historic Raghunath Temple and Ranbireshwar Temple. The Mubarak Mandi Palace reflects a blend of Rajasthani, Mughal, and European architectural styles.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, with its historic Gulmarg Biosphere Reserve, offers a picturesque setting. The Gulmarg Gondola is one of the highest cable cars in the world, providing stunning views.
