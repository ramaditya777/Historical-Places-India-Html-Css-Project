Historical Places in Nagaland

  • Kohima
  • Hornbill Festival
  • Dzukou Valley

Kohima

Kohima, the capital, holds historical significance from the Battle of Kohima during World War II. Visit the Kohima War Cemetery and State Museum.

Hornbill Festival

The Hornbill Festival is a celebration of Naga culture and traditions. It takes place annually in Kisama and showcases indigenous art, music, and dance.

Dzukou Valley

Dzukou Valley is known for its enchanting landscapes and trekking trails. It offers a serene retreat amidst lush greenery and colorful flowers.
