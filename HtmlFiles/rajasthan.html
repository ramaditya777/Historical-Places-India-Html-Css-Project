Historical Places in Rajasthan

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Amer Fort

Amer Fort, also known as Amber Fort, is a majestic fort located in Jaipur. Explore the intricate architecture, stunning palaces, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, or the Palace of Winds, is an iconic palace in Jaipur. Admire the unique honeycomb design and learn about its historical significance in Rajasthan's royal history.

City Palace

City Palace in Udaipur is a splendid complex of palaces, courtyards, and gardens. Immerse yourself in the grandeur of Rajput architecture and discover the rich cultural heritage.
