Historical Places in Assam

  • Kaziranga National Park
  • Sivasagar - Rang Ghar
  • Majuli Island
  • Kamakhya Temple

Kaziranga National Park

Kaziranga National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to the one-horned Indian rhinoceros, tigers, elephants, and various bird species.

Sivasagar - Rang Ghar

Sivasagar, once the capital of the Ahom Kingdom, features historical sites like Rang Ghar, a two-story building used for cultural events and sports during Ahom rule.

Majuli Island

Majuli, the world's largest river island, is known for its unique culture, Vaishnavite monasteries (Satras), and vibrant festivals.

Kamakhya Temple

Kamakhya Temple, dedicated to the goddess Kamakhya, is a revered Hindu pilgrimage site atop Nilachal Hill in Guwahati.
