Historical Places in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

  • Dudhani Lake
  • Diu Fort
  • Silvassa Tribal Museum

Dudhani Lake

Dudhani Lake, located in Dadra and Nagar Haveli, is a serene water reservoir surrounded by lush greenery. Enjoy boating, nature walks, and the peaceful ambiance of this picturesque lake.

Diu Fort

Diu Fort, situated in the union territory of Daman and Diu, is a historic Portuguese fort overlooking the Arabian Sea. Explore the fort's architecture, cannons, and panoramic views of the coastline.

Silvassa Tribal Museum

Silvassa Tribal Museum, located in the capital of Dadra and Nagar Haveli, showcases the rich tribal heritage of the region. Explore artifacts, traditional crafts, and learn about the indigenous cultures.
