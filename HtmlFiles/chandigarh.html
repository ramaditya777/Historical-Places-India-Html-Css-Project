Historical Places in Chandigarh

  • Rock Garden
  • Sukhna Lake
  • Zakir Hussain Rose Garden

Rock Garden

Rock Garden is a unique sculpture garden created by Nek Chand using industrial and home waste. Explore the labyrinth of pathways adorned with sculptures made from recycled materials.

Sukhna Lake

Sukhna Lake is an artificial reservoir at the foothills of the Himalayas. Enjoy a serene boat ride, take a leisurely stroll along the promenade, and appreciate the scenic beauty of the lake.

Zakir Hussain Rose Garden

Zakir Hussain Rose Garden is the largest rose garden in Asia, housing a vast variety of roses. Explore the beautifully landscaped garden, featuring fountains, sculptures, and over a thousand rose varieties.
