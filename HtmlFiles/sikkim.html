Historical Places in Sikkim

  • Rumtek Monastery
  • Nathula Pass
  • Tashiding Monastery

Rumtek Monastery

Rumtek Monastery, also known as the Dharmachakra Centre, is a Tibetan Buddhist monastery located near Gangtok. Explore the spiritual ambiance and intricate Tibetan architecture.

Nathula Pass

Nathula Pass, a high mountain pass on the Indo-China border, holds historical significance. Learn about its role in the Silk Road and its importance during the Indo-China conflicts.

Tashiding Monastery

Tashiding Monastery, perched on a hilltop, is one of the oldest monasteries in Sikkim. Experience the serene surroundings and delve into the rich cultural heritage of Sikkim.
