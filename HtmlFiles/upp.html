Historical Places in Uttar Pradesh

  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri

Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is an iconic white marble mausoleum in Agra. Explore the symmetrical gardens, intricate architecture, and the romantic history behind this masterpiece.

Agra Fort

Agra Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage site, is a historic fort in Agra. Discover the red sandstone architecture, royal palaces, and the stunning views of the Taj Mahal from the fort.

Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri, a city built by Emperor Akbar, is known for its architectural grandeur. Explore the Buland Darwaza, Diwan-i-Khas, and the historical significance of this abandoned city.
