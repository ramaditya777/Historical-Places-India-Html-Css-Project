Auroville

Auroville is an experimental township known for its focus on unity and spiritual development. Visit the Matrimandir, explore the international community, and experience the unique atmosphere of Auroville.

Paradise Beach

Paradise Beach, also known as Plage Paradiso, is a pristine beach with golden sands and clear blue waters. Relax by the sea, enjoy water activities, and experience the natural beauty of this coastal paradise.
