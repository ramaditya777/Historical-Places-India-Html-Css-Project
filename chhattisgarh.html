Historical Places in Chhattisgarh

  • Bastar Palace
  • Chitrakoot Falls
  • Tirathgarh Falls
  • Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary

Bastar Palace

Bastar Palace, located in Jagdalpur, is a historical monument known for its unique architecture and cultural significance. It was the residence of the rulers of Bastar princely state.

Chitrakoot Falls

Chitrakoot Falls, also known as Niagara Falls of India, is a natural waterfall on the Indravati River. It is a picturesque spot surrounded by lush greenery.

Tirathgarh Falls

Tirathgarh Falls is another beautiful waterfall in Chhattisgarh, known for its stunning cascade amidst dense forests. It's a popular tourist destination.

Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary

Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. It houses diverse flora and fauna, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers.
